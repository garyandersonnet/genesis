APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 17: The Flood
==================================================

[Verse 1]

Yaldabaoth looked down at the race of the light —
And saw that the sparks were too many, too bright.
The humans were waking — the gnosis was spread —
He plotted to wash out the knowing with dread.

He called for a counsel in his heavens above —
He planned the great flood to destroy what he'd loved
To rule and to hold — but now would resist —
He raged at the light he could barely exist.

[Chorus]

But Pronoia — the divine Forethought — came down —
She whispered to Noah: "Don't let yourself drown.
The flood is coming — the waters will rise —
Build the vessel — and carry the wise."

[Verse 2]

Not a boat made of gopher — not a hull made of wood —
But a vessel of light in the way that it should —
A vessel of knowledge — the gnosis the ark —
A brightness of spirit to survive through the dark.

The flood came and covered the world of the blind —
But those who had kept the true light in their mind
Were carried above on the waters below —
Preserved in the knowing that continues to flow.
