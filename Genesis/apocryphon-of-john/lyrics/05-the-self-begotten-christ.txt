APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 5: The Self-Begotten Christ
==================================================

[Verse 1]

Barbelo gazed at the Father in love and desire —
And from their mutual gazing there sprang up a fire.
A spark of pure knowing — a seed of the light —
The Self-Begotten One blazed into sight.

The Father was pleased and He poured out His grace —
He anointed the Son with the light of His face.
Christos — the Anointed — established on high —
The first-born of all things beneath the bright sky.

[Chorus]

Autogenes — self-born — the only-begotten —
A light before morning — a word not forgotten.
He stood in the fullness and praised the great Name —
And the Father anointed Him — He was the same.

[Verse 2]

Four companions were given to stand at his side —
Four angels of grace through the light-river wide.
He perfected them each in the truth of his mind —
And over the aeons his glory was signed.

The Self-Begotten looked down through the layers of light —
And the lower world shivered at the edge of his sight.
He who came from the Father and Barbelo combined —
The Christ of the Gnosis — the first of his kind.
