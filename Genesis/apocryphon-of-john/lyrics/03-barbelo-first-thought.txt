APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 3: Barbelo — First Thought
==================================================

[Verse 1]

The Invisible Spirit gazed into its own light —
And out of that gazing came the first night
That was not darkness but knowing — the first of all thought —
The forethought of God — the first thing He wrought.

Barbelo arose like a mirror of the One —
The Mother of all before the world had begun.
She stood in the light and she said with a voice:
"Grant me foreknowledge — let this be my choice."

[Chorus]

Barbelo — First Thought — the womb of the light —
The triple-named Mother of the eternal night.
She praised the Father — the invisible ground —
And the universe shivered with the echo of sound.

[Verse 2]

"Grant me indestructibility — grant me truth —
Grant me eternal life — give me eternal youth."
And at each of her askings the Spirit said yes —
And the pleroma expanded in glory and bless.

She is the first power — before all the rest —
The mother-aeon — the holy — the blest.
She looked to the Father and he looked to her —
And from that eternal gaze — all things were stirred.
