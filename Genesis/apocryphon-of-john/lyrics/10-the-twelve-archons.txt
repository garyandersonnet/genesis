APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 10: The Twelve Archons
==================================================

[Verse 1]

Yaldabaoth stretched out his power below —
And called forth twelve rulers to govern the low.
He fashioned them each from the fire in his chest —
And gave each dominion — from east to the west.

Athoth and Harmas — Kalila-Oumbri —
Yabel and Adonaiou — the powers of three.
Sabbataios and Sabaoth and Adonin —
He set them like stones in the walls of his kingdom.

[Chorus]

Seven great heavens — twelve archons of fate —
Each one a warden at a different gate.
They govern the planets and bend the soul's will —
Binding the living to the cosmic treadmill.

[Verse 2]

He breathed in each archon a portion of fire —
And bound them to rule through the centuries' choir.
Each one took a face — each one took a name —
And each one administered ignorance and shame.

They are the powers behind every false throne —
The rulers who make every exile feel alone.
But above the twelve heavens the real light shines clear —
And the Invisible Spirit was never not here.
