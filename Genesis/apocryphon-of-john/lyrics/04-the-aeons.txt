APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 4: The Aeons
==================================================

[Verse 1]

Barbelo conceived from the Father's own will —
A spark from the gazing — a light to fulfil.
She brought forth the thought and it blazed in the deep —
The realm of the aeons awakened from sleep.

Foreknowledge and Incorruption stood in the light —
Eternal Life spread like a river of white.
And Truth was enthroned at the Father's own side —
A fivefold perfection — the aeons' full tide.

[Chorus]

The pleroma fills — the fullness expands —
With powers and glories and light-bearing bands.
Each aeon a note in the song of the whole —
Each light-bearer singing the great pleroma's soul.

[Verse 2]

The Will and the Word and the Mind intertwined —
In the upper-most dwelling of the upper-most mind.
Grace came and Beauty and Understanding and Bless —
And the realms of the Father grew full to excess.

These are the twelve — these are the thirty —
The infinite circles of the good and the worthy.
A cosmos of spirit in radiant array —
Before the first darkness — before the first clay.
