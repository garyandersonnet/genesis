APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 6: The Four Luminaries
==================================================

[Verse 1]

From the throne of Autogenes four lights arose —
The four holy servants — four luminous glows.
Harmozel the first stood in joy and in grace —
In remembrance and love she held up her face.

Oroiael came second — in concept and mind —
And memory and will were the gifts left behind.
Daveithai came third — understanding and grace —
And love held the third of the luminous space.

[Chorus]

Eleleth the fourth — the last and the deep —
Perfection and peace — and the knowledge to keep.
Four pillars of heaven — four thrones of the light —
The four corners of glory before the long night.

[Verse 2]

In Harmozel's dwelling the Adam of Light was placed —
And Mirotheos and the Self-Begotten embraced.
In Oroiael the souls of the holy ones sit —
In Daveithai the holy seed shall be lit.

And Eleleth the great — who holds the last age —
Within her the fallen ones wait on the stage.
The four great luminaries hold creation in frame —
Four fires of the eternal and holy great Name.
