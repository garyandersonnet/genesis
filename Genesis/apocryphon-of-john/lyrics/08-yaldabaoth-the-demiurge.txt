APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 8: Yaldabaoth — The Demiurge
==================================================

[Verse 1]

Sophia's thought fell into the cloud and the dark —
And moved on the waters — a misshapen spark.
It grew in the shadows of chaos below —
A lion-faced serpent with a terrible glow.

He opened his eyes and he saw only night —
For he had no share in the upper world's light.
He moved through the darkness and called it his home —
A god of the bottom — a king made of stone.

[Chorus]

Yaldabaoth — the blind one — the jealous and proud —
Who rules from the dark underneath a thick cloud.
He took from his mother the power she bore —
And left her diminished — and hungry for more.

[Verse 2]

He stole from Sophia the light that she gave —
And used it to build an impermanent grave.
A cosmos of matter — of heaviness and weight —
A prison of forgetfulness — governed by fate.

He is the maker of everything visible here —
The seven-fold heavens of ignorance and fear.
He calls himself God but his eyes have not seen
The infinite light of the realm where he's been.
