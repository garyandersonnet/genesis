APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 15: The Spiritual Eve
==================================================

[Verse 1]

The Epinoia of Light was hidden in Adam —
The power of waking — the key to the pattern.
The archons could see that the man had a light —
They could not extinguish — a flame in the night.

God cast a deep sleep on the Adam below —
Not slumber of rest but a forgetting-low.
And while he lay dreaming, the power was drawn —
The Epinoia of Light — like a veil — like a dawn.

[Chorus]

She rose from his side as the living light-Eve —
Not made from a rib — but woven to breathe
Life back into Adam — to wake what had slept —
The spiritual woman — the promise still kept.

[Verse 2]

When Adam awoke and he opened his eyes —
He saw her and knew her — he saw through disguise.
"You are bone of my bone — you are light of my light —
You are the one who can wake me from night."

For she was Zoe — the Life — and the Mother —
The hidden instructor — the sister and other.
The Epinoia of Light wearing Eve's face —
Come down to the garden to restore what had grace.
