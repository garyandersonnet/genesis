APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 13: The Light Enters Adam
==================================================

[Verse 1]

Sophia looked down at the form in the clay —
And begged for the light to restore what was grey.
The Father had mercy — He sent from on high
The Epinoia of Light — a breath — a reply.

She descended as spirit into Adam's dark frame —
And breathed the first knowing into the clay man's name.
The body that lay there unmoving and blind
Suddenly blazed with the fullness of mind.

[Chorus]

He rose — and the archons fell back in their fear —
For the thing they had made now surpassed what was here.
Their own creature shone with a light they'd not made —
And Adam stood upright in the celestial grade.

[Verse 2]

The archons saw this and were filled with dismay —
Their prisoner glowed with the light of the day.
They could not contain the one light that had come —
The spark of Sophia — the seed of the sun.

They took him and cast him into the deep ground —
The garden of matter — where forgetting is found.
But the light in the body could not be undone —
The divine spark sleeping — the secret of One.
