APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 18: The Counterfeit Spirit
==================================================

[Verse 1]

Yaldabaoth saw that the flood had not won —
The sparks still were waking beneath the dark sun.
He called on his angels and said in his rage:
"Make for each human a spirit — a cage."

A spirit of counterfeit — fashioned to bind —
To fill up the body and darken the mind.
A spirit of forgetting — of lust and of sleep —
A chain made of pleasure — a promise to keep.

[Chorus]

They sent it down into the bodies of men —
A mirror of spirit — a forgetting again.
It whispers of pleasures — it drowns out the call —
The voice of the knowing — the light underneath all.

[Verse 2]

This is the spirit that drives the world's wheel —
The hunger that never has enough meal.
The voice that says: "This is all that there is —
The body is real and the world — only this."

But beneath the false spirit the true spark remains —
A prisoner sleeping in ignorance's chains.
And one day the spark will remember its name —
And the counterfeit spirit will go back to flame.
