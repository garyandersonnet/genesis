APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 19: The Fate of Souls
==================================================

[Verse 1]

John asked: "What becomes of the soul when it dies —
Does it rise to the light — does it fall and not rise?"
The Saviour said: "Listen — three paths there will be —
Three destinies written — three ways to be free."

The first is the soul that receives the true light —
That wakens and knows and returns to the bright.
It rises through all of the archons' own doors —
And reaches the Father on the heavenly shores.

[Chorus]

The second soul wavers — it turns and then falls —
It goes back to the body again in the halls.
It's given more chances — more lifetimes to turn —
More chances to remember — more chances to learn.

[Verse 2]

The third is the soul that has given its all
To the counterfeit spirit — has answered its call.
Who loved only darkness and clung to the clay —
When the body dissolves — it is carried away

By the archons and cast to a place of despair —
A soul that chose binding — no Pronoia there.
But still the great question hangs over the deep:
Which path do you walk — and which do you keep?
