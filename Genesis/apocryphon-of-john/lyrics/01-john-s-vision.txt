APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 1: John's Vision
==================================================

[Verse 1]

John stood at the temple in grief and alone —
The Pharisee's question cut deep to the bone:
"Where has your teacher gone? Where did he go?
The one you called master — what did he know?"

John turned from the temple and fled to the hill,
A wilderness mourning, a sorrow so still.
He wrapped himself in the dark of the place —
When the heavens split open and blazed with a face.

[Chorus]

The light came in threefold — young, old, and the same —
A single great presence that answered his name.
"John — why do you doubt? Why do you fear?
I am the one who was with you — I'm here."

[Verse 2]

"I am the Father, the Mother, the Son —
The incorruptible one — and I am the one
Who has come to teach you what is, what was —
What shall be in the age of the world without cause."

The vision descended in luminous flame —
A teacher returning — but not as he came.
Not a body of flesh but a radiance of knowing —
A secret now open, a river now flowing.
