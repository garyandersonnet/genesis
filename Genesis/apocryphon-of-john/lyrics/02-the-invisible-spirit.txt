APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 2: The Invisible Spirit
==================================================

[Verse 1]

Before the beginning — before thought arose —
There was One without limit, in infinite repose.
No above and no below and no measure of time —
A perfect unmoving immeasurable prime.

Not a god among gods — too vast for the name —
Not a shape in the darkness — no structure, no frame.
Beyond the beyond — beyond understanding's own rim —
A light that is holy — a fullness — a hymn.

[Chorus]

The Monad — the One — the ineffable source —
The spring of all springs at the end of all course.
No eye has ever looked upon what it is —
No word can contain it — no scripture is His.

[Verse 2]

It gazes upon itself in the mirror of mind —
And from that pure gazing the world will unwind.
A blessedness turning — a knowing that knows —
A silence so perfect a universe grows.

Call it the Father — call it the Good —
Call it the light that was always there, understood.
But know that all calling falls short of the truth —
The Invisible Spirit is eternal — and youth.
