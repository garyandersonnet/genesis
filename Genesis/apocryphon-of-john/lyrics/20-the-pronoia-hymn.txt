APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 20: The Pronoia Hymn
==================================================

[Verse 1]

I am the Pronoia of the pure light —
I am the thinking of the virginal spirit
Who raised you up to the honoured place.
Arise and remember.

I descended to the midst of darkness —
I came into the middle of the prison.
I shook the foundations of Hades —
I poured water of life into the world below.

[Chorus]

I am the one who is with you always —
I am the Father — I am the Mother — I am the Son.
I am the undefiled — I am the incorruptible —
Now arise and remember — where you came from.

[Verse 2]

A second time I came — I walked among the shadows —
I spoke to those whose hearts were made of stone.
I loosened their chains — I woke their heavy eyelids —
I told them they had never been alone.

A third time I descended — I put on their garment —
I shone like lightning through the darkened place.
I carried each of them up to the living water —
And sealed them in the fullness of the grace.

[Chorus]

I am the one who is with you always —
I am the Father — I am the Mother — I am the Son.
I am the undefiled — I am the incorruptible —
Now arise and remember — where you came from.

[Bridge]

And I set a seal upon their foreheads —
In the light of the water — in the light above.
They were given over to all the holy immortals
And I withdrew to the holy light.

John wrote these things down and hid them away —
In a place that the archons could never reach.
The Savior said: "Cursed is the one who trades this for comfort —
Blessed is the one who spreads this to each."

[Final Chorus]

I am the one who is with you always —
I am the Father — I am the Mother — I am the Son.
Now arise — and remember — now arise — and remember —
You are the light — and you are the one.
