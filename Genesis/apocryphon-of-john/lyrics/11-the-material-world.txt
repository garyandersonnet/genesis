APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 11: The Material World
==================================================

[Verse 1]

The archons were called to their building below —
Each one assigned the materials they'd know.
Three hundred sixty-five angels were sent
To fashion a body — a copy — a tent.

From fire and water and wind and from earth —
From bitterness, darkness, and the false kind of birth —
They made a material cosmos of weight —
A copy of heaven — a shadow-world — late.

[Chorus]

A world that was made from forgetting and night —
A veil pulled between every soul and the light.
The firmament set and the waters below —
A magnificent prison — a glittering show.

[Verse 2]

They made the great rivers — they charted the seas —
They wove into being the grass and the trees.
But none of it held any spark that could wake —
Just matter on matter — just echo and ache.

For a copy of heaven reflects what is true —
The light that is hidden in every bright dew.
The material world is a lens — not a wall —
If you know how to look — the light shines through all.
