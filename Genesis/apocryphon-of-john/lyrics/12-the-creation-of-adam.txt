APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 12: The Creation of Adam
==================================================

[Verse 1]

A voice came from above the highest heaven —
A voice like a thunder in a sevenfold leaven:
"The Human exists! The Anthropos is here!"
And the image shone down through the water so clear.

The archons looked upward and saw in the deep
A form of great radiance — a beauty asleep —
An image of the light-world that flickered above —
And they said to each other: "Let us make this one of."

[Chorus]

Each archon contributed — bone after bone —
A body of clay and a body of stone.
Three hundred sixty-five angels brought their part —
A vessel of matter with no beating heart.

[Verse 2]

They laid the form on the ground — and it couldn't arise —
No light in the body — no spark in the eyes.
It lay in the darkness like a shadow of man —
A form without spirit — no breath, no life's plan.

For they made what they saw but could not understand —
The light that had passed through the water's own hand.
They built a new cage — but forgot what they'd seen —
The image of heaven caught between and between.
