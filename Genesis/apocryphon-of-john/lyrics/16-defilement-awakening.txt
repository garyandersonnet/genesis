APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 16: Defilement & Awakening
==================================================

[Verse 1]

The archons saw Eve — and they burned with desire —
They wished to possess what was lit up with fire.
They tried to defile her — to drag down the light —
To smother the knowing and restore the long night.

But the true Eve — the spiritual — stepped out of the reach —
She laughed at the archons from beyond their beseeched.
They could only touch shadow — they only held clay —
The light was not captive — the light slipped away.

[Chorus]

They planted their own lie inside what remained —
A counterfeit spirit — a forgetting-maintained.
But the seed of the knowing was already sown —
In Adam, in Eve, in the world they had grown.

[Verse 2]

And Yaldabaoth came with a face like a beast —
He used what he'd stolen from Sophia's feast.
From what he had touched — two sons were then born —
Eloim and Yawe — the tree was now torn.

Yet in the children who carry the spark —
A fragment of Sophia burns bright in the dark.
The defilement could not reach the innermost light —
The spirit inside walked on, undimmed through the night.
