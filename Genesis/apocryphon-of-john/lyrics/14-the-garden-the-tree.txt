APOCRYPHON OF JOHN
Section 14: The Garden & the Tree
==================================================

[Verse 1]

The archons placed Adam inside the great garden —
A beautiful prison — designed to harden
The heart against knowing — the mind against sight —
A place where forgetting would keep out the light.

Two trees were among them — the one and the two —
The tree of their rule — and the other that grew
Not as the archons had planted — but shining and bright —
The Epinoia of Light — the tree of true sight.

[Chorus]

"Eat not of the knowing — eat not of the wise —
Or you'll certainly die," said the archons' own lies.
But the tree of true knowledge was the Christ in disguise —
Come to wake what was sleeping — to open blind eyes.

[Verse 2]

For the tree in the garden was not what they said —
Not a danger to life — but a waking from dead.
The archons had planted their tree of control —
But the real tree was given to free every soul.

The voice in the garden that said "Do not eat"
Was Yaldabaoth's own voice — a covering deceit.
For he feared that the man would remember his birth —
And rise from the garden — and leave this false earth.
